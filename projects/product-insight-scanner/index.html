Project

Product Insight Scanner (Yuka-style)

Scan product data with clear health and transparency insights.

Overview

Product Insight Scanner is a consumer-focused application that helps users evaluate everyday products by scanning and analyzing ingredient and nutritional data. The challenge was translating complex and inconsistent product information into a format that is easy to understand. The solution involved structuring data from external APIs into a clear scoring system and visual interface that highlights health, sustainability, and ingredient transparency.

Key Features

  • Barcode Scanning

    Supports quick lookup of product records through barcode input.

  • Health Scoring

    Converts ingredient and nutrition details into a readable score.

  • Ingredient Transparency

    Highlights additives and ingredients in plain language.

  • Sustainability Signals

    Surfaces useful product context beyond basic nutrition values.

App

Highlights

  • Readable Data Experience

    Presents technical product data as actionable consumer insights.

  • Fast Evaluation Flow

    Reduces friction from scan to decision with concise visual summaries.

  • Strong Assignment Framing

    Demonstrates API integration, scoring logic, and polished UX communication.
