Product Insight Scanner (Yuka-style)

Scan barcodes to get health, sustainability, and quality insights at a glance.

App screenshot or mockup placeholder

Rationale

This project was built to explore how product transparency tools can help consumers make informed choices without overwhelming them. The scanner draws on open datasets and a Yuka-style experience: quick scans, clear scores, and concise explanations. The goal was to demonstrate a clean, accessible flow from barcode input to actionable insight, with room to extend into sustainability and nutrition breakdowns. It serves as a portfolio piece that shows both front-end polish and sensible API integration.

Key Features

  • Barcode scanning

    Enter or scan product barcodes to instantly look up product data from open food and consumer databases.

  • Health & quality scores

    Simple, visual scores and badges so users can quickly assess product quality and nutritional impact.

  • Transparent breakdown

    Ingredient lists, additives, and sustainability cues presented in plain language with minimal clutter.

Tech Stack

  • React
  • Node.js
  • Open Food Facts API
  • REST API
