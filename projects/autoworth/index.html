Overview

AutoWorth is a car listing analysis tool that helps users determine whether a vehicle is worth buying, negotiating on, or avoiding based on key factors like price, mileage, and condition.

Key Features

  • Value Score System

    See a clear score that summarizes how the listing stacks up at a glance.

  • Buy / Negotiate / Avoid Recommendation

    Get a direct recommendation so decisions stay simple and confident.

  • Price vs Market Insight

    Understand how the asking price compares to typical market expectations.

  • Mileage Analysis

    Factor in odometer readings relative to age and use patterns.

  • Risk Indicators

    Spot red flags in condition, history, or listing details before committing.
