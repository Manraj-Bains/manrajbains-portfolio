Overview

PracticePlanner is an intelligent workout planning tool that generates structured training splits and provides progression guidance, recovery insights, and adaptive recommendations based on user input.

Process

The project began with user research around how people structure weekly training and where they lose consistency. Wireframes and flows were iterated to prioritize split planning and progression visibility before implementation, with ongoing refinement based on usability feedback.

Key Features

  • Weekly Split Planner

    Build and adjust training splits by day and focus area so each week stays organized and goal-aligned.

  • Progression Timeline

    See how volume and intensity evolve over time to stay on track and avoid plateaus.

  • Recovery Insights

    Surface recovery cues and spacing so training stays sustainable and injury-aware.

  • Adaptive Recommendations

    Get suggestions that respond to user input and performance so plans stay relevant.
